Every Game, Every Week

40,000 sims/game · Vegas-anchored means & win probability · offseason edition

How to read this. Each game is simulated 40,000 times. The two things that are real: the team means come from the posted Vegas implied totals, and the spread calibrates the win probability (a −7.5 favorite wins ~72% here because that is what the market implies). Everything shaped on top — the spread of outcomes (SD 9.5, ceiling-modulated), the two teams' co-movement (ρ rising with the total), and the pass/run script shifts — are stated priors anchored to NFL-empirical dispersion, not fit to 2026 results (none exist yet). So trust the ordering and the shape; treat any single percentage as "what these assumptions imply," not a measured frequency. The distribution is shown first; the most-likely script read follows it.
